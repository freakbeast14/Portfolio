Connect is an online platform catering to teachers and students. It enhances engagement by monitoring students' attention and automating attendance based on response times. The app facilitates screen sharing, notifies students about classes, and offers teachers quick attendance reports. It provides insights into student engagement by tracking app status, making it a comprehensive solution for seamless online education.
